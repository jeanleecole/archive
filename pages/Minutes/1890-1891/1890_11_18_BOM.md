## Board Meeting November 18th 1890.
[Book I, MS 988, Box 3, p. 19]

### Board meeting; Mode of election of new members; Committee proposed

Meeting of the Board of Management was held at 861 Garden Street, Tuesday afternoon, November 18th(?) at 3:30 oâ€™clock, President in chair, all members present. Subject taken up of mode of election of new members, submission of paper (original) by proposed member suggested as test of admission--also suggested that Committee be formed to interview members to be proposed and see if they wish to join the Club. Decision postponed. Mrs. Lake [Margaret Lake] elected. Adjourned.

Eliza Ridgely
Secretary
